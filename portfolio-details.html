The Team

Victoria Naematu Nadolie Abdulai

Founder & President

Ms. Abdulai is a vastly experienced marketing and management professional. She currently serves as the CEO of TelNaima Advertising and Executive Director of The Practical Sales College. Ms. Abdulai has also held several high-profile positions in the past including as CEO/Director of Gold and Diamond House Co. Ltd, Marketing Executive of Marcot Surgical, and Events Coordinator of Smart Multimedia, Accra. Her résumé boasts of a vast array of accomplishments related to successfully organizing events, conferences and logistical management. These include for entities such as the Namibian High Commission, the Department of Trade and Industry of the African Union Commission (AUC) and the African Union Election Observer Mission to Ghana for which she provided logistics support during the 2016 elections. She also been an electoral observer for the African Union in Nigeria (2015), Namibia (2014), Ethiopia (2014) and Mozambique( 2019).

Sammy Okudzeto Ablakwa. Mr Adiamah is keen on leadership and advancement, with clarity in communication and ensuring that success is achieved in every endeavor.
